## 🎌 **JAPANESE ELEMENTS EXPLAINED**

### **Kanji Characters Used:**

1. **武** (bu)
   - Means: "Warrior" or "Martial"
   - On the seal badge
   - Traditional martial arts symbol

2. **道** (dō)
   - Means: "Way" or "Path"
   - In title as accent
   - Philosophical concept in martial arts

3. **武道** (budō)
   - Means: "Martial Way" or "Martial Arts"
   - Complete traditional term
   - Outline stroke style
